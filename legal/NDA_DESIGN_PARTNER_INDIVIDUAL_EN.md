## PREAMBLE

STOA develops an open source API management platform ("STOA Platform") and wishes to benefit from feedback from industry professionals.

The Design Partner agrees to test the platform and provide feedback in exchange for early access to features under development.

The Parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1 — PURPOSE

This agreement defines the confidentiality terms applicable to information exchanged under the STOA Platform Design Partner program.

---

## ARTICLE 2 — CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

### 2.1 Definition

The following constitutes "**Confidential Information**":

- Non-public product roadmap
- Unannounced features under development
- Internal architecture not publicly documented
- Strategic and commercial discussions
- Internal benchmark and performance data
- Any information expressly designated as confidential

### 2.2 Exclusions

The following is **NOT** considered confidential:

- Source code published under Apache 2.0 license
- Public documentation available at docs.gostoa.dev
- Publicly announced features
- Information already known to the Design Partner prior to signing
- Information that has become public without fault of the Design Partner

---

## ARTICLE 3 — DESIGN PARTNER OBLIGATIONS

The Design Partner agrees to:

### 3.1 Confidentiality
- Not disclose Confidential Information to third parties
- Not reproduce or copy Confidential Information except as necessary for evaluation
- Protect Confidential Information with the same degree of care as their own confidential information

### 3.2 Limited Non-Compete
- Not use Confidential Information to develop, directly or indirectly, a competing product for a period of **twelve (12) months** from the date of signature
- This clause does **not** apply to use of public source code under the Apache 2.0 license

### 3.3 Return of Information
- Return or destroy Confidential Information upon STOA's request
- Confirm destruction in writing

---

## ARTICLE 4 — DESIGN PARTNER RIGHTS

The Design Partner is authorized to:

### 4.1 Usage
- Freely use the public version of STOA Platform
- Test early-access features made available

### 4.2 Feedback
- Provide feedback, suggestions, and criticism
- Such feedback may be incorporated into the product **without financial compensation**
- The Design Partner waives any intellectual property rights over incorporated suggestions

### 4.3 Communication
- Publicly mention their status as "STOA Design Partner" **with prior written consent**
- Participate in testimonials or case studies **with prior written consent**

---

## ARTICLE 5 — DURATION

### 5.1 Program Duration
This agreement enters into force on the date of signature and remains in effect for **six (6) months**, renewable by tacit renewal.

### 5.2 Confidentiality Obligations Duration
Confidentiality obligations survive the end of the program for **two (2) years**.

### 5.3 Termination
Either Party may terminate this agreement with **fifteen (15) days** written notice.

---

## ARTICLE 6 — LIABILITY

In case of breach of this agreement, the Design Partner may be held liable for direct damages caused to STOA, limited to the actual and proven harm suffered.
